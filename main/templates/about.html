About Our College Venue Booking System

Hall Booking System

Our College Hall Booking System is a convenient and efficient platform designed to simplify the process of reserving rooms and halls for your events and activities within our college campus.

Key Features

  • Streamlined Booking: Say goodbye to the hassle of waiting for faculty approval. You can now request venues online, anytime, from anywhere.
  • Approval Workflow: Your booking requests will be reviewed online, and once approved, they become legitimate reservations.
  • Institute Account Login: Access the system with your institute account for a secure and seamless experience.

How It Works

  1. Log in with your institute account credentials.
  2. Select the venue and date you want to reserve for your event.
  3. Submit your request, which will be reviewed by the designated authority.
  4. Once approved, your reservation is confirmed, and you can start planning your event!
